Personalized Study Schedule

It is essential to follow a disciplined study schedule. Here's a simple breakdown:

  • Morning (7 AM - 10 AM): Focus on Physics (Mechanics, Thermodynamics)
  • Midday (11 AM - 1 PM): Chemistry (Organic and Inorganic Chemistry)
  • Afternoon (3 PM - 5 PM): Mathematics (Algebra, Calculus)
  • Evening (7 PM - 9 PM): Revision and Practice Problems

Adjust according to your needs and subjects. Stick to a daily routine for better results.
